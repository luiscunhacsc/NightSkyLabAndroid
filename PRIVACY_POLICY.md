# Privacy Policy for NightSkyLab

**Last updated:** February 1, 2026

## Overview

NightSkyLab is an astronomy application that displays the night sky based on your location. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### Location Data
- **What we collect:** GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
- **Why:** To calculate accurate star and planet positions for your location
- **Storage:** Location data is processed locally on your device only
- **Sharing:** We do not transmit, store, or share your location data

### No Other Data
- No personal information collected
- No account registration required
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising

## Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Play Services Location API to obtain your device's GPS coordinates. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.

## Data Security

All astronomical calculations are performed on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
